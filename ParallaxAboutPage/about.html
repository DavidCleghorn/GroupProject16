About RetroVG

About us

Started in 2022, we began with a mission to provide quality games for an affordable price. Whether you're looking at games for nostalgia, wanting to play great games from gaming history, or looking to experience something different, we will have something for you.

Our project

The truth of the matter though is that we made this project for Generation USA. We had a group project to make a website of our choosing. Our group decided on a website that sells vintage video games: something that we love.

While it wasn't easy, we were able to put our growing skills together to fashion this website. The website itself showcases the skills we learned while taking part in this program. Even though some had worked in the tech industry before, others were starting from scratch with "Hello World". This is the culmination of us pulling together as a team and creating something from scratch. We hope you enjoy the site and look at our individual portfolios to learn more
