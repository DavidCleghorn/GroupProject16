 About RetroVG 

New to you

Started in 2022, we began with a mission to provide quality games for an affordable price. Whether you're looking at games for nostalgia, wanting to play great games from gaming history, or looking to experience something different, we will have something for you.

First Project For Generation USA

We started Generation in June of 2022 and shortly thereafter started our first challenge, known simply as group 16. 

Our project was to create a working website that could showcase the skills that we have learned. In doing so, we had to study, practice, and work well as a team to communicate the many needs we would have.

In doing so, we were able to create a working site by fusing together a mixture of ideas from 6 individuals, all the while pursuing our passion in web development. We hope you enjoy the site.
